Who We Are

Rooted in Georgia's Hazelnut Heritage

Located in Zugdidi, Samegrelo, AgroProfile has been processing premium hazelnuts since 2011. We're deeply connected to Georgia's top hazelnut-growing region, sourcing the finest raw materials from both our own plantations and trusted local farms.

Led by Archil Iobashvili, our team of 30 professionals ensures every step of production meets the highest standards — all backed by HACCP certification for food safety and quality.

How We Work

Our Production Process

01
Harvesting

We select only the finest hazelnuts from our own plantations and trusted local farms in Georgia's premier growing region.

02
Cleaning & Drying

Hazelnuts are thoroughly cleaned and dried using modern equipment to ensure optimal freshness and quality.

03
Sorting

We sort and grade every batch so only hazelnuts meeting our strict standards advance to the next stage.

04
Cracking

Precision technology cracks the nuts while maintaining their structural integrity and natural quality.

05
Roasting

Our roasting process enhances the natural flavor, bringing out the rich, deep taste Georgian hazelnuts are known for.

06
Grinding & Packaging

Carefully ground and packaged to maintain freshness — ready for delivery to clients around the world.
